About Me

I am a research applicant dedicated to addressing the critical challenge of legibility in large language models. My work aims to bridge the gap between pure learning-based systems and symbolic reasoning to build more transparent and accountable AI. I focus on developing robust frameworks for calibrated uncertainty, selective abstention, and rationale logging. My methodology emphasizes full reproducibility, utilizing containerized environments (Docker), deterministic seeding, and artifact hashing to ensure the reliability and verifiability of my findings.

Key Project Output
